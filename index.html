    • DISTANCE LEARNING INSTITUTION

    The Distance Learning Institute (DLI) is the organ of the University that operates and coordinates the Open and Distance Learning programmes. It has an enhanced status as that of a faculty in the University. It has its own Management Board and an Academic Board. It is empowered to formulate its own policies, employ its own staff and be responsible for academic programmes, examinations, supervise its day-to-day affairs and generate funds for its operations.

    INSTITUTE OF CONTINUING EDUCATION

    It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the Institute of Continuing Education (ICE) University of Lagos (The University of First Choice and the Nation’s Pride). The Institute of Continuing Education (ICE) was established by Senate of the University in 2009 to coordinate all the undergraduate degree programmes, run on part-time basis in the various Faculties of the University. The Institute currently coordinates the Sandwich degree programmes of the Faculty of Education, Faculty of Arts degree programmes,Sociology and Social Work degree programmes of Faculty of Social Sciences (evening). These programmes lead to the award of B. A. (Hons.) Edu., B.Sc. (Hons.) Edu., B. A. (Hons.) and B.Sc. (Hons.) degrees. Lagos, being a mega-city, has a lot of people whose circumstances require that they combine work with studies.
